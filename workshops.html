What We Do

Bloom'n Events Co. delivers hands-on workshop experiences that spark curiosity and connection. From single-day activations to full school holiday programmes across Perth and Western Australia—nature art, sustainability, food crafts, and creative learning.

Designed and delivered in-house. No external facilitators to brief. Customised to your event, season, or theme. We've delivered workshops for Hawaiian Shopping Centres and other leading Perth businesses—driving traffic and engagement.

Suitable for kids, adults, seniors, and team-building. We provide all materials and facilitation. One team. Consistent quality. No coordination headaches.

How It Works

1. Book Your Workshop

Contact us to discuss your needs, audience, dates, and location. We'll recommend suitable workshop activities and provide a customised quote.

2. We Prepare Everything

Our team prepares all materials, tools, and supplies. We handle setup, ensuring everything is ready before participants arrive.

3. Facilitate the Session

Our experienced facilitators guide participants through each activity, ensuring everyone feels supported and engaged throughout the workshop.

4. Clean Up & Follow-Up

We handle all pack-down and cleanup. Participants take home their creations, and we're available for feedback and future bookings.
