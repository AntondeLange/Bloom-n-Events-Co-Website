A World of Flavours: Community Event & Retail Activation Positions Forrestfield as Community-Driven Destination

How Bloom'n Events Co delivered a vibrant family-focused activation celebrating international food offerings, driving awareness to Taste of Italy and creating memorable community moments

Context

Client

Hawaiian Group | Forrestfield
Community Event & Retail Activation

The Brief

Hawaiian Group engaged Bloom'n Events Co. to deliver a vibrant, family-focused activation celebrating the international food offering at Hawaiian's Forrestfield, while driving awareness and visitation to the newly opened Taste of Italy.

The event formed the live centrepiece of a broader two-week digital "A World of Flavours" campaign, designed to increase customer engagement, boost food retailer spend, and create a memorable community moment.

The Challenge

The activation needed to:

  • Attract families and increase dwell time
  • Encourage participation without disrupting centre flow
  • Integrate seamlessly with a digital loyalty campaign
  • Deliver strong visual impact within a limited outdoor footprint

Most importantly, it had to feel joyful, inclusive, and authentic—without becoming chaotic or cluttered.

Process

The Solution

Bloom'n Events Co. transformed The Junction outdoor space into a lively, international alfresco village—layering hands-on creativity, cultural performance, and sensory storytelling into a single cohesive experience.

  • Children were invited to participate in make-your-own food craft stations, choosing between pizza, taco, or gelato creations
  • Each child stepped into character as a "mini chef," wearing personalised chef hats and aprons—turning the activity into playful role-play rather than simple craft
  • Finished creations were exchanged at a European-style voucher redemption food cart, linking the experience directly back to participating food retailers

The Experience

  • One-day live event within a two-week digital campaign
  • Continuous kids' craft workshops (300 children in 5 hours)
  • Cultural performances including African drumming, Latin dance, and folk music
  • Roving entertainers in traditional European attire
  • Live international DJ set by DJ Neenz, creating a relaxed, multicultural soundscape
  • Italian-inspired alfresco styling with umbrellas, bunting, props, and dining tables

The space felt immersive yet controlled—energetic without overwhelming families or surrounding retailers.

Services Delivered

Event concept & strategy · Community placemaking · Kids activations · Cultural entertainment · Styling & props · DJ & soundscape curation · Onsite delivery & coordination

The Outcome

A World of Flavours delivered outstanding results:

  • Strong family attendance throughout the day
  • High participation rates across all kids' activity stations
  • Increased engagement with participating food retailers
  • Strong uptake of vouchers and digital campaign participation
  • Positive feedback from centre management, retailers, and customers

The event successfully positioned Hawaiian's Forrestfield as a community-driven destination, not just a shopping centre.

Key Insight

When experiential marketing connects creativity, culture, and commerce, it builds more than foot traffic—it builds loyalty. A World of Flavours demonstrated how thoughtful placemaking can translate brand objectives into joyful, measurable outcomes for both customers and retailers.
