Food Festival Event Celebrates Tenant Opening and Drives Community Engagement

How Bloom'n Events Co created an engaging one-day activation for Hawaiian Shopping Centres at Forrestfield

The Challenge

Client: Hawaiian Shopping Centres
Location: Hawaiian Forrestfield, Perth
Objective: Create a one-day event that celebrated the opening of a key tenant while highlighting the diversity of food offerings at the shopping centre.

The brief required creating a fun and engaging family-friendly activation that would encourage dwell time and participation. The event needed to serve multiple purposes: celebrate the new tenant, showcase the centre's food diversity, and create a memorable experience for both retailers and the community.

Our Approach

We designed a food festival experience that combined interactive elements with beautiful presentation. The event featured food demonstrations, tastings, and activities that engaged families while naturally showcasing the variety of food options available at the centre.
