What We Do

Bloom'n Events Co. delivers immersive corporate experiences designed to connect, elevate, and inspire. From single-day activations to multiday retreats, our events span networking, professional development, leadership, and brand engagement across Perth and Western Australia.

We design thoughtfully curated environments that empower professionals to connect, collaborate, learn, and thrive together. From dynamic B2B showcases and immersive executive training sessions to bespoke branded activations, every event is carefully tailored to reflect your team's unique culture, core message, and strategic objectives.

Based in Brookton, WA, we serve businesses throughout Perth and regional Western Australia, bringing 40+ years of combined expertise in event planning, design, and technical execution to every project.

How It Works

1. Consultation

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your objectives, audience, budget, and vision. We'll discuss your goals and explore how we can bring your event to life.

2. Design & Planning

Our team creates a comprehensive event plan including layout, styling, technical requirements, and timeline. We handle all design, logistics, and coordination in-house.

3. Execution

On event day, our team manages setup, technical elements, styling, and coordination. We ensure everything runs smoothly so you can focus on your guests and objectives.

4. Follow-Up

After the event, we handle pack-down and provide a summary. We're available for feedback and to discuss future events or ongoing partnerships.
