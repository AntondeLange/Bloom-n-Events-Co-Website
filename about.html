About Bloom'n Events Co

Crafting Moments That Move People

A family-founded company driven by purpose and passion. We bring connection, joy, and beauty to every corner of Western Australia through inspired, sustainable event experiences.

Get in Touch View Our Work

Who we were...

Bloom'n Events Co. began in 2018 under the original name The Ridge Retreat & Health Gardens, delivering creative, nature-based workshops for children, teens, and adults.

We've worked across Perth, from Midland Gate's Fresh Food Precinct to seasonal activations at Belmont Forum, Stockland Baldivis and City of Swan's Avon Descent Festival. One highlight was the 12-day "Reuse, Repurpose, Recycle" programme, drawing families back with rotating sustainability activities. Post-COVID, we've re-emerged more focused and driven—ready to transform WA spaces through connection, creativity, and community.

Where we are...

Bloom'n Events Co. is a passionate and experienced team delivering engaging event activations across WA. Our work blends wild crafts, art, gardening, food, and sustainability into accessible experiences for people of all ages.

With over 40 years of combined experience in retail management, casual leasing, agribusiness, placemaking and local Government, we know how to bring energy and community to even the most underused spaces. We're proud collaborators, customising every event to reflect local needs and values.

Local knowledge. Local partnerships. One team that understands your community.

Vision

To bring connection, joy, and beauty to every corner of Western Australia — transforming moments into memories through inspired, sustainable event experiences.

Mission

We're a family-founded company driven by purpose and passion. We craft immersive artistic activations and design unforgettable events that uplift, connect, and give back. By working with other local businesses, with the same creed and supporting regional communities, we prove that business success and compassion can grow side by side.

Where we are headed...

Bloom'n Creative Design Co. is the next chapter—Western Australia's boutique design studio for eye-catching displays and immersive installations.

Our work spans corporate launches, festivals, weddings, and retail displays. From our Wheatbelt studio, we design and construct large-scale, reusable sets and custom props—engineered for visual impact, sustainability, and transportability. We deliver installations that inspire joy and elevate spaces.
