What We Do

At Bloom'n Creative Design Co. we create unique, custom-designed displays that leave lasting impressions. Specialising in large, lifelike installations, we bring creative flair and visual impact to corporate events, public celebrations, seasonal holidays, and venue launches across Perth and Western Australia.

Each installation is thoughtfully crafted to reflect your brand identity or event theme, combining artistic innovation with meticulous attention to detail. We've created displays for Centuria Capital, Hawaiian Shopping Centres, and other leading Perth businesses.

Whether you need seasonal displays, event installations, or permanent displays, we create solutions that captivate and inspire your audience.

How It Works

1. Consultation

We discuss your vision, venue, theme, and objectives. Our designers work with you to create a concept that perfectly matches your needs.

2. Design & Build

Our in-house team designs and builds your display, sourcing materials and creating unique, oversized installations with grand visual impact.

3. Installation

We handle complete setup, ensuring seamless venue integration. Our team manages all technical elements, lighting, and styling.

4. Maintenance & Pack-Down

We provide ongoing maintenance throughout your event and handle complete pack-down afterward. For hire displays, we manage storage and reuse.
