Connect 140: B2B Tenant Networking Event Repositions 140 St Georges as Premier Business Hub

How Bloom'n Events Co delivered a curated B2B sundowner that transformed a building address into a connected commercial community

Context

Client

Centuria | 140 St Georges Terrace
B2B Tenant Networking Event | Perth CBD

The Brief

Centuria set out to host the first Business-to-Business networking event for tenants at 140 St Georges Terrace—bringing together senior executives from across finance, legal, engineering, government, and professional services.

Objective: Create a premium, memorable experience that strengthened relationships, fostered collaboration, and positioned 140SGT as the premier business hub on the terrace.

The Challenge

With a diverse tenant mix—many both collaborators and competitors—the event needed to go beyond traditional networking. It had to feel sophisticated yet relaxed, purposeful yet engaging, and valuable enough to justify senior leaders' time.

Process

The Solution

Bloom'n Events Co. delivered Connect 140—a curated B2B sundowner designed as an interactive business ecosystem rather than a passive cocktail event.

  • The open-plan foyer was transformed into a refined networking arena using a black, white, and gold palette aligned with Centuria's brand
  • Furniture was removed to maximise flow, visibility, and interaction
  • A series of Giant Connect Four activations became the event's strategic centrepiece—breaking down barriers, prompting conversation, and turning introductions into meaningful exchanges
  • Every element was designed to encourage connection without compromising professionalism

The Experience

  • ~100 executives in attendance from finance, legal, engineering, government, and professional services
  • Premium canapés, grazing stations, and curated beverages
  • Branded company profile displays to prompt targeted introductions
  • Structured interactive gameplay supported by branded conversation cards
  • Post-event QR engagement for continued connection

Services Delivered

Event strategy · Experiential design · Branding & collateral · Interactive activations · AV & catering coordination · Onsite delivery · Post-event engagement

Visual Evidence

Our Approach

In-House Production & Engineering

Every element of Connect 140 was designed, engineered, and produced in-house—from concept sketches to final installation. This end-to-end control ensured precision, quality, and seamless execution.

  • Design & Engineering: Custom Giant Connect Four games engineered for stability, safety, and visual impact in a corporate environment
  • Fabrication: All branding, signage, and interactive elements built locally in our workshop
  • Technical Planning: Detailed site analysis, flow mapping, and safety assessments conducted in-house
  • Installation & Delivery: One team managed setup, execution, and breakdown—ensuring consistency and accountability

Engineering-Grade Reliability

With 40+ years of combined expertise from high-risk, high-compliance environments, we applied engineering precision to every detail—from structural integrity of custom installations to safety protocols and contingency planning. Corporate and government clients trust us because we deliver with the same rigor and reliability they expect from their own operations.

Results

Val Neal

Customer Service Manager, One Forty St Georges

The Outcome

Connect 140 delivered immediate and lasting impact:

  • High engagement across the full event duration
  • Strong cross-industry conversations and new business connections
  • Overwhelmingly positive guest feedback
  • Clear demand for future Centuria-hosted B2B events

The event successfully repositioned 140SGT as more than a premium address—it became a connected commercial community.

Key Insight

When networking is designed with intent, it becomes a business asset. Connect 140 demonstrates how experiential strategy can elevate a building's identity, strengthen tenant relationships, and create long-term value—far beyond a single evening.
