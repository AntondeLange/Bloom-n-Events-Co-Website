Corporate Networking Event Delivers Meaningful Connections for Centuria Capital

How Bloom'n Events Co created an engaging corporate networking experience at One Forty St Georges, Perth

The Challenge

Client: Centuria Capital
Location: One Forty St Georges, Perth
Objective: Create a corporate networking event that would bring together the business community in a meaningful, engaging way.

Centuria Capital needed a networking event that would stand out from typical corporate gatherings. The brief required creating an atmosphere that would naturally encourage conversation, connection, and engagement among Perth business professionals.

Our Approach

We designed an event that combined intentional spatial layouts with atmospheric styling to create natural conversation zones. Rather than traditional rows of tables, we created varied seating arrangements and interactive spaces that encouraged movement and organic interaction.

The design incorporated custom floral arrangements and displays that reflected Centuria's brand while creating visual interest throughout the venue. Every element—from the stage setup to the dining areas—was planned to facilitate connection rather than passive attendance.

Results

"We couldn't have done it without you. Everyone loved the concept."

Val Neal, Customer Service Manager, One Forty St Georges

The event successfully created the engaging atmosphere Centuria Capital sought. Guest feedback highlighted the thoughtful design and how the layout naturally facilitated meaningful conversations. The combination of professional styling with intentional spatial planning delivered an experience that felt both polished and approachable.
