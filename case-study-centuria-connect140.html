B2B Networking Event Delivers Meaningful Connections for Centuria Capital

How Bloom'n Events Co created an engaging B2B corporate networking experience at 140 St Georges, Perth

Context

Client

Centuria Capital, a leading Australian real estate investment manager, operating 140 St Georges (140SGT) in Perth.

Goals

  • Create a B2B corporate networking event that would bring together the Perth business community
  • Design an atmosphere that naturally encourages conversation and meaningful connections
  • Stand out from typical corporate gatherings with intentional design and styling
  • Deliver a professional yet approachable experience that reflects Centuria's brand

Constraints

  • Location: 140 St Georges, Perth - existing venue with specific layout requirements
  • Audience: Perth business professionals requiring an environment conducive to B2B networking
  • Timeline: Event delivery within standard corporate event planning timeframe
  • Brand Alignment: Design needed to reflect Centuria Capital's professional brand identity

Process

What We Did

We designed and delivered a B2B corporate networking event that combined intentional spatial layouts with atmospheric styling to create natural conversation zones.

  • Created varied seating arrangements and interactive spaces instead of traditional rows of tables
  • Designed custom floral arrangements and displays that reflected Centuria's brand
  • Planned every element—from stage setup to dining areas—to facilitate connection rather than passive attendance
  • Coordinated full event management including setup, styling, and on-site execution

Why This Approach

Traditional corporate event layouts often create barriers to conversation. By designing spaces that encourage movement and organic interaction, we created an environment where meaningful B2B connections could happen naturally. The combination of professional styling with intentional spatial planning ensured the event felt both polished and approachable.

Deliverables

  • Complete event design and spatial planning
  • Custom floral arrangements and displays
  • Full event setup and styling
  • On-site event management and coordination
  • Professional photography documentation

Results

"We couldn't have done it without you. Everyone loved the concept."

Val Neal, Customer Service Manager, 140 St Georges

Outcomes

  • Successfully created the engaging atmosphere Centuria Capital sought for their B2B networking event
  • Guest feedback highlighted the thoughtful design and how the layout naturally facilitated meaningful business conversations
  • The combination of professional styling with intentional spatial planning delivered an experience that felt both polished and approachable
  • Event achieved its objective of bringing together the Perth business community in a meaningful way
