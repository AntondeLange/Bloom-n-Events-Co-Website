School Holiday Programme Drives Traffic and Family Engagement Across Seven Shopping Centres

How Bloom'n Events Co delivered a successful food-based activity programme for Hawaiian across their suburban neighbourhood assets

The Challenge

Client: Hawaiian Pty Ltd
Location: Seven suburban shopping centres across Perth
Objective: Create a food-based July school holiday activity that would drive traffic, encourage dwell time, and engage families across multiple locations.

The programme needed to be scalable across seven different shopping centres while maintaining consistency in quality and experience. The activities needed to appeal to families—Hawaiian's most valuable market segment—while being logistically feasible to deliver across multiple locations.

Our Approach

We developed the "Neighbourhood Nibbles" programme—a food-based activity that combined hands-on learning with creative engagement. The programme was designed to be portable, consistent, and adaptable to different centre layouts while maintaining the same high-quality experience at each location.

Each activity was carefully planned to be both fun and educational, encouraging children to participate while parents could engage or observe. The food theme naturally connected with the shopping centre environment while providing a unique experience that families wouldn't find elsewhere.

Results

"Hawaiian engaged Bloom'n Events Co to run a food-based July school holiday activity across seven of our suburban neighbourhood assets. The activity was not only unique but beautifully presented, with every detail considered. Commercially, the activities drove traffic uplifts and encouraged dwell amongst our most valuable market segment – families. We would not hesitate to engage the Bloom'n team again."

Sarah Moore, Portfolio Marketing Manager, Hawaiian Pty Ltd

The programme successfully achieved its commercial objectives. Traffic uplifts were recorded across the participating centres, and the activities effectively encouraged families to spend more time at the shopping centres. The consistent quality and unique presentation of the activities created a memorable experience that differentiated Hawaiian's centres during the competitive school holiday period.
