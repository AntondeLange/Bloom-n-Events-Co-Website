School Holiday Programme Drives Traffic and Family Engagement Across Five Shopping Centres in 5 Days

How Bloom'n Events Co delivered a successful food-based activity programme for Hawaiian across their suburban neighbourhood assets

Context

Client

Hawaiian Pty Ltd
Five suburban shopping centres across Perth

The Challenge

Create a food-based July school holiday activity programme that would:

  • Drive traffic uplifts across five different shopping centres
  • Encourage dwell time among families—Hawaiian's most valuable market segment
  • Engage families with hands-on, fun, and educational activities
  • Be scalable and portable while maintaining consistent quality across all locations
  • Be logistically feasible to deliver across multiple centres within a 5-day timeframe

Process

The Solution

We developed the "Neighbourhood Nibbles" programme—a food-based activity that combined hands-on learning with creative engagement.

  • Designed to be portable, consistent, and adaptable to different centre layouts
  • Maintained the same high-quality experience at each location despite varied spaces
  • Each activity was carefully planned to be both fun and educational
  • Encouraged children to participate while parents could engage or observe
  • Food theme naturally connected with the shopping centre environment while providing a unique experience

The Experience

  • Food-based July school holiday activity programme
  • Delivered across five Hawaiian shopping centres in 5 days
  • Hands-on, creative engagement activities that appealed to families
  • Scalable programme maintaining consistency in quality and experience
  • Unique presentation with every detail considered

Visual Evidence

Results

"Hawaiian engaged Bloom'n Events Co to run a food-based July school holiday activity across five of our suburban neighbourhood assets in 5 days. The activity was not only unique but beautifully presented, with every detail considered. Commercially, the activities drove traffic uplifts and encouraged dwell amongst our most valuable market segment – families. We would not hesitate to engage the Bloom'n team again."

The Outcome

The programme successfully achieved its commercial objectives:

  • Traffic uplifts were recorded across all five participating centres
  • The activities effectively encouraged families to spend more time at the shopping centres, increasing dwell time
  • The consistent quality and unique presentation created a memorable experience that differentiated Hawaiian's centres
  • Successfully delivered across five centres in 5 days, demonstrating scalability and efficient execution
  • Programme appealed to families—Hawaiian's most valuable market segment

Key Insight

When a programme is designed with scalability and consistency at its core, it can successfully drive measurable commercial outcomes across multiple locations. The Neighbourhood Nibbles programme demonstrated that portable, high-quality activations can effectively engage target audiences and deliver traffic uplifts while maintaining the flexibility to adapt to different centre environments.
